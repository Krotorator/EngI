Our mission is to facilitate venues with a functional platform that will enhance fan experience while increasing revenue. SFDC will challenge the traditional hospitality approach from the exclusive to all-encompassing by delivering a fully accessible service. The App

Using our application you will receive notifications about different events. All recommendations based on your location, preferences and events which have been liked by you or your friends. It's easy to find any kind of avocation for your weekend. Go out, explore, appreciate art, learn and get inspired. We’re aimed on helping you with searching for tickets quickly, so you can focus on enjoying life and that is the matter!
